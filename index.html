About Me

When I'm not pretending to be an adult, I enjoy travel and spending time with my adult daughters. However, I can usually be found now with my laptop open and like a moth to the flame, me attracted to blue glow coming from screen.

So basically, still a kid staying up way too long past their bedtime.

Like many of those pretending to be adults, I go to work each day looking forward to being challenged and putting my Superman cape on to tame the chaos that my co-workers enjoy creating.

I enjoy being the calm in the storm and rising up daily to meet and exceed the demands created by those around me who have become spoiled by my anticpating their every want and need. Well at work anyway.
